Getting started

A complete introduction to Onshape CAD by designing a fully-functional model rocket.


We recommend a split-screen layout:

{% include step_counter.html %} Set up windows in split view

  • Use Windows key + Left Arrow to move one browser to the left
  • Use Windows key + Right Arrow to move the other browser to the right
  • Consider making the tutorial thinner than the CAD window so more of the controls are fully visible in the view.

{% include step_counter.html %} Create a Hobbyist onshape account

  • Head to the create free account page at Onshape

  • Input your:
    • First Name
    • Last Name
    • Email Address
    • Press Next

  • On page 2: What do you want to use Onshape for
    • Select Fun
    • Press Next

  • On page 3: What best describes you?
    • Select Hobbyist
    • Press Next

  • On page 4: Confirm your Plan
    • Press Next

  • On page 5: Add your details
    • Phone number: Your mobile phone number
    • What CAD system are you most familiar with: Onshape
    • Where are you in your CAD evaluation process: Browsing / General Interest
    • Press Next

  • On page 6: Complete your signup
    • Do you use CAD at work: No
    • How did you hear about us: Stage One Education
    • Check acknowledgements
    • Press Complete Sign Up

  • Open your email and click the link we sent to activate your account
    • Press Activate Your Account
