3D Printng Workflow

  1. Design in CAD: Completed
  2. Export as STL: Creates a file of tiny triangles (a “point cloud”) that describe your model’s surface.
  3. Import into Slicer: Pick material, layer thickness, and other print settings.
  4. Generate G-Code: The slicer turns your design into precise instructions for the 3D printer.
  5. Start Printing: Load the G-Code into your printer and watch as it builds your part layer by layer.
