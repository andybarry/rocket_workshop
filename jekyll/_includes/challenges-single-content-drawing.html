  • Select the “Create Drawing” option.
  • Choose the desired sheet size and standard (e.g., ISO, ANSI) for your drawing.
  • Place the base view of your part, then add additional views (e.g., top, side, isometric) as needed.
  • Add dimensions, notes, and any necessary annotations to clearly communicate your design.
