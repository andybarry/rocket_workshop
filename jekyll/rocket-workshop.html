Overview
3-3.5 Hours | All Ages | No Experience Needed

In the Mechanical Engineering Workshop, participants become rocket designers!

  • Design and engineer your own three-part rocket using industry-standard Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.

  • Guided by GIF‑style micro‑videos and concise text, no prior experience is required.

  • Master core CAD skills: sketching, parametric modeling, assemblies.

  • Live 3D‑printing demo.

  • Learn from open‑forum Q&A where you can ask our real engineers anything about your design, CAD techniques, and careers in engineering.
